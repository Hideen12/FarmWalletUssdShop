## One-Liner (Elevator Pitch)

FarmWallet is a USSD platform for Ghana that lets anyone — on any phone — buy rice from verified shops, book mechanization services (tractor, plowing, threshing), and make VSLA savings contributions, all via mobile money. No app, no internet, no smartphone required.

## Target Audiences & Key Messages

### Rice Shops (Sellers)
**Who:** Smallholder farmers, traders, rice exhibitors  
**Message:** Turn your phone into a shop. Register with Ghana Card, add your rice, get paid via MoMo. Each shop gets its own shortcode — customers dial straight to you.  
**CTA:** Dial *920*72# → Register as Shop

### Buyers (Consumers)
**Who:** Households, retailers, institutions  
**Message:** Browse verified rice shops, compare prices, pay securely with MTN, Vodafone, or AirtelTigo MoMo. No cash, no hassle.  
**CTA:** Dial *920*72# → Browse Shops & Buy Rice

### Farmers (Mechanization)
**Who:** Farmers needing tractor, plowing, threshing, harvesting  
**Message:** Book tractor and farm equipment services. Enter your acres, get instant pricing. Connect directly with providers.  
**CTA:** Dial *920*72# → Mechanization Services

### Mechanization Providers
**Who:** Tractor owners, plowing services, equipment rental businesses  
**Message:** Get a dedicated shortcode. Farmers find you, book services, pay per acre. Track earnings on the web dashboard.  
**CTA:** Contact admin to register → Get your *920*72*XX# code

### VSLA Members (Farmers, Leaders, VBAs)
**Who:** Village Savings and Loan Association members  
**Message:** Check your profile, view groups and savings, make contributions via MoMo — all from your phone. No need to meet in person to contribute.  
**CTA:** Dial *920*72# → VSLA - My Profile

## FAQs (For Customer Support / Marketing)

**Q: Do I need a smartphone?**  
A: No. Any phone that can make calls can use USSD. Dial *920*72# and follow the menu.

**Q: Which mobile money networks are supported?**  
A: MTN, Vodafone, and AirtelTigo.

**Q: How do I register my rice shop?**  
A: Dial *920*72# → 1. Register as Shop. You’ll need your Ghana Card, business name, and MoMo number.

**Q: How do I make a VSLA contribution?**  
A: Dial *920*72# → 6. VSLA - My Profile → Make Contribution. Select your group, enter amount, pay with MoMo.

**Q: Is there a web dashboard?**  
A: Yes. Shop owners and mechanization providers can log in at the FarmWallet web portal to manage inventory and view earnings.
